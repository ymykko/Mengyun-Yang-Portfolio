Brand Identity

KACO Brand Identity System

A self-directed rebranding project for KACO, a Chinese stationery brand. The project rebuilds the brand’s visual identity through research, logo exploration, colour system, typography, tone of voice, and brand guideline design.

Project Overview

From brand research to a complete identity system.

The project responds to KACO’s limited brand recognition, weak brand story, and inconsistent visual elements across platforms. It aims to build a more distinctive, energetic, and internationally adaptable identity system while keeping the brand connected to stationery, creativity, and everyday inspiration.

The final guideline defines the brand story, mission, vision, values, tone of voice, logo system, colour palette, typography, packaging direction, and website concept.

My Role

Independently led research, design exploration, and final documentation.

I was responsible for the full process, including early-stage brand research, competitor analysis, visual direction exploration, logo design, colour system development, typography selection, brand guideline layout, and production log documentation.

Design Direction

A more playful, energetic, and memorable KACO.

The redesigned identity uses bold shapes, high-contrast colours, and playful visual elements to move KACO beyond a purely minimal stationery image. The slogan “KO Challenges, Action Now” reinforces a creative, optimistic, and action-driven brand tone.
